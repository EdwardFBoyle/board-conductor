## What Board Conductor Does

Board Conductor evaluates any company — startup or established — through four lenses:

| Lens | Core Question |
|---|---|
| **Wedge** | Is the company starting in the right narrow market? |
| **Learning Engine** | Is the company learning faster than competitors? |
| **Data Laboratory** | Is early traction becoming reusable knowledge? |
| **Board Conductor** | Is the board asking the right questions? |

The framework combines two ideas:

**Clayton Christensen** — disruption begins in overlooked markets; a company proves the product at a wedge segment and moves upmarket before incumbents notice.

**Karim Lakhani** — in the AI era, competitive advantage is built not just through better products but through faster learning systems. AI-native companies instrument every interaction, automate experimentation, and compound knowledge at a rate traditional companies cannot match with headcount.

The board question shifts from *Are we missing the disruptor?* to *Are we learning fast enough to matter?*

## How to Use a Skill

Each skill file contains ready-to-paste prompts. Three usage modes:

**Mode 1 — Upload a deck**: Paste the deck review prompt into any AI assistant and attach your pitch deck, investor deck, or board packet.

**Mode 2 — Upload a business plan**: Use the business plan prompt for longer documents — strategic memos, board packets, growth-round plans.

**Mode 3 — Interactive session**: Use the interactive prompt with no document. The AI interviews you and produces a board-level summary.

Every skill produces the same output structure:
- Executive diagnosis
- Four-lens assessment (Wedge / Learning Engine / Data Laboratory / Board Conductor)
- Scorecard (7 dimensions, 1–5)
- Risk flags
- Top board questions
- 30/60/90-day action plan
