Christensen × Lakhani
AI Strategy Review

Are we learning fast enough to discover, prove, and scale the right wedge?

A board-level diagnostic combining Clayton Christensen's disruption theory with Karim Lakhani's AI-era reframe of competitive advantage. Paste it into any AI — Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini — and attach your deck or business plan.

Four Lenses

1
Wedge
Are we discovering or proving the right narrow market?
ICP clarity, urgency signals, market pull, early repeatability
2
Learning Engine
Is the company learning faster than competitors?
Feedback loops, signal capture, experimentation cadence, AI leverage
3
Data Laboratory
Is early traction becoming reusable knowledge?
Data assets, workflow documentation, playbooks, automation
4
Board Conductor
Is the board asking the right questions?
Decision quality, risk flags, scaling readiness, AI governance

Output

  • Executive diagnosis — Discovery Mode or Execution Mode, and why it matters
  • Wedge Assessment — current wedge, strength, weakness, recommended refinement
  • Learning Engine + Data Laboratory assessments
  • Scored rubric across 7 dimensions (out of 35)
  • 12 risk flags — named, defined, with board questions for each
  • 7 board questions specific to this company
  • 30/60/90-day action plan
